Mid-Term Test, Chapter 1 - 12

Sunday, December 20th 2020

1. The__________is one God in three Persons.

  1. Holy Trinity
  2. Holy Spirit
  3. Holy Family

2. God the Father is the_________who made everyone and everything out of love.

  1. Apostle
  2. Creator
  3. Holy Spirit

3. Jesus is the___________ Person of the Holy Trinity.

  1. First
  2. Second
  3. Third

4. The part of us that lives forever is called the __________.

  1. heart
  2. mind
  3. soul

5. We honor the Holy Trinity when we pray the __________.

  1. Hail Mary
  2. Sign of the Cross
  3. Lord's Prayer

6. Who is God the Holy Spirit?

  1. Savior
  2. Creator
  3. Helper

7. God made a solemn_____________with his people.

  1. Pentecost
  2. mercy
  3. covenant

8. We call Jesus' dying on the cross the ______________.

  1. Crucifixion
  2. faithful
  3. Baptism

9. God sent the Holy Spirit on the day of _____________.

  1. body of Christ
  2. Pentecost
  3. Jesus

10. We first receive the Holy Spirit in the Sacrament of _________.

  1. Baptism
  2. confirmation
  3. Ascension

11. The Church is also called the _______________.

  1. communion of Saints
  2. People of God
  3. Pentecost

12. God the Father raising Jesus from the dead to new life is called ____________.

  1. the Crucifixion
  2. the Resurrection
  3. mercy

13. The virtue that helps us to act with kindness toward others no matter what is __________.

  1. mercy
  2. the Faithful
  3. covenant

14. The Seven_________are signs of God's love for us.

  1. Bibles
  2. Sacraments
  3. Prayers

15. Water and oil are used in the Sacrament of _________.

  1. Baptism
  2. Penance and Reconciliation
  3. Matrimony

16. Jesus told the story of the forgiving ________ to teach us about God's forgiveness.

  1. Son
  2. Brother
  3. Father

17. In the Sacrament of Confirmation we receive_________.

  1. Jesus
  2. Spiritual Gifts
  3. forgiveness

18. ________ is freely choosing to do or say something we know God does not want us to do or to say.

  1. Penance
  2. Reconciliation
  3. Sin

19. ____________ strengthens us by the Holy Spirit.

  1. Baptism
  2. Confirmation
  3. Sin

20. ___________ forgives sin committed after Baptism.

  1. Spiritual Gift
  2. Matrimony
  3. Penance and Reconciliation
